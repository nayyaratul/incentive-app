# Toast

Temporary, non-blocking notifications that appear at the edge of the viewport. Built on Radix UI, it requires wrapping your app with `ToastProvider` and placing a `ToastViewport`.

<Primary />

<Controls />

## Variants

Each variant conveys a different level of urgency.

- **Default** — general notifications.
- **Success** — confirms a completed action.
- **Error** — something failed.
- **Warning** — caution or advisory information.

<Canvas of={ToastStories.Variants} />

## With Action

Include a `ToastAction` to let users act on the notification, such as undoing a destructive operation. The `altText` prop describes the action for screen readers.

<Canvas of={ToastStories.WithAction} />

## Accessibility

- Built on `@radix-ui/react-toast`, which manages focus and announces content to screen readers.
- Close button includes `aria-label="Close"`.
- `ToastAction` requires an `altText` prop for assistive technology.
- Supports swipe-to-dismiss on touch devices.
